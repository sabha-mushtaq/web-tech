Beautiful Destinations of Kashmir

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a serene hill station in Jammu and Kashmir. It offers trails to Amarnath Cave Temple, views of Lidder River, and the scenic Betaab Valley surrounded by snow-clad peaks.

Aru Valley

Aru Valley is a small yet stunning village famous for its scenic meadows, pristine beauty, and as a starting point for trekking expeditions and camping adventures.

Betaab Valley

Named after a Bollywood movie, Betaab Valley is famous for lush greenery, crystal-clear streams, and breathtaking views, making it a must-visit destination in Kashmir.

Tulian Lake

Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, Tulian Lake is a high-altitude lake often frozen. It is a hidden gem for trekkers and adventure seekers visiting Kashmir.
